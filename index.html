The Centre for Grower-centric Eco-value Mechanisms
is developing a platform for ecosystem markets based
on natural farming.
Transitions to natural farming are tough and take time.

We are in an ongoing climate crises which will affect smallholder farmers the most.

The transition from conventional to Natural Farming can take up to 8 years

Extreme events like droughts, excess rainfalls, heat wave can destabilize the transition

Transition to Natural Farming will require additional financial support for the farmers
to incentivize them to take up the practices and sustain them

C-GEM is supporting changemakers for long-term and sustainable behavior shifts
by leveraging innovative funding from ecosystem markets.
